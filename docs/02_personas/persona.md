# Persona: Social Padel Player

### Name: Lucas Slack

 Age: 26

 Location: Urban area

 Occupation: Young professional

Padel experience: Beginner to intermediate

Frequency of play: 1–2 times per week

    Context
    Lucas discovered padel recently through friends and social media. He enjoys the accessibility of the sport and its strong social dimension. He mainly plays after work or on weekends. While motivated, he finds it hard to find partner every time and seek and all in one padel app. 

### Goals

+ Play padel regularly without organizational trouble

+ Find players of similar skill level nearby

+ Easily join or create games

+ Track progress in a simple and motivating way

+ Discover relevant padel content, clubs, and events

### Motivations

+ Social interaction and shared activity

+ Fast enjoyment and low entry barrier

+ Visible progression and recognition

+ Belonging to a local sports community

+ Convenience and time efficiency

### Behaviors

+ Uses messaging apps to organize matches

+ Uses a separate app or website to book courts

+ Consumes padel-related content on Instagram or TikTok

+ Occasionally shares match results or photos

+ Prefers mobile-first, intuitive interfaces

### Pain Points

+ Too many disconnected apps for one activity

+ Difficulty finding available players at the right time

+ Uncertainty about other players’ skill levels

+ Performance tracking that lack or is too pricey in the form of a coach

### Needs

+ A single platform covering all padel related content

+ Integrated social and booking features

+ Simple and clear skill indicators and matchmaking

+ Local-first discovery of games, players, and clubs

+ A modern, engaging, content-driven experience
